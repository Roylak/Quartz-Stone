FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our quartz products and ordering process

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
For standard quartz slabs, our MOQ is 50 square meters (about 10 slabs). For custom countertops and washbasins, MOQ starts from 10 pieces. Sample orders are also available for quality testing.
How long is the production lead time?
Standard slab orders are typically shipped within 10-15 days after deposit. Custom countertops and special colors take 15-20 days. We also keep popular colors in stock for immediate delivery.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept T/T (50% deposit in advance, 40% balance against copy of Bill of Lading, 10% balance to be paid after goods arrive at the destination port), L/C at sight, and Trade Assurance through Alibaba. For sample orders, PayPal and Western Union are also acceptable.
Can I get samples before ordering?
Yes, we provide free small samples (10x10cm or 15x15cm) for quality and color verification. You only need to cover the express shipping cost. Sample delivery takes 3-5 days via DHL/FedEx.
Do you offer custom sizes and edge profiles?
Absolutely! We offer custom cutting and various edge profiles including bullnose, bevel, ogee, flat polish, and more. Just send us your drawings or specifications for a custom quote.
What certifications do your products have?
Our quartz products meet CE, NSF, and ISO 9001 international quality standards. We also provide SGS test reports for hardness, stain resistance, and food safety upon request.
